# Church Directory

## Product Purpose
A welcoming digital directory that serves both new visitors seeking churches and existing congregation members. The site helps people discover churches, pastors, sermons, and spiritual content while fostering community connection.

## Users
- **New visitors** — Searching for a church home; need easy navigation, clear information, and inviting atmosphere.
- **Existing congregation members** — Checking sermons, event schedules, pastor information, and community resources.
- **Church administrators** — Managing church listings, pastor profiles, sermon uploads, events, and community books.

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Warm and welcoming** — Feels like genuine community connection, not corporate sterility.
- **Approachable** — Clear language, no jargon; accessible to all ages.
- **Authentic** — Real stories, real people, genuine faith focus.
- **Community-centered** — Emphasizes connection and belonging over marketing.

## Color Strategy
Currently uses a restrained palette:
- Dark top bar (#1a242f)
- Cream background (#f4eee1)
- Red accent (#ee6352) for CTAs
- Green logo text (#4a6d55)

**Strategy**: Committed — the warm cream and natural greens should carry the visual identity while red accents guide key actions (Join Us, Learn More, etc.).

## Anti-References
- Corporate/sterile church sites (cold blues, corporate sans-serif)
- Overly modern tech vibes (loud gradients, neon)
- Cluttered, hard-to-navigate layouts
- Auto-playing videos or intrusive pop-ups
- Poor mobile experience for on-the-go users

## Strategic Principles
1. **Hospitality first** — The site itself should feel welcoming before any content.
2. **Content clarity** — Sermon archives, church listings, and pastor profiles must be easy to scan and discover.
3. **Mobile native** — Many users discover churches on mobile; every page must work flawlessly.
4. **Community over promotion** — Celebrate member stories, events, and connection.
5. **Accessibility** — Different ages, vision abilities, tech comfort levels.

## Design Register
**product** — The design serves the community connection purpose; it's not the main product itself.

## Key Pages to Optimize
- **Homepage** — First impression; should communicate warmth and community
- **Church finder/Churches** — Discovery experience for new visitors
- **Pastor profiles** — Personal connection and trust
- **Sermon archive** — Content discoverability and listening experience
- **Admin dashboard** — Efficient management of content

## Current Strengths
- Thoughtful color palette with warm tones
- Logical information architecture (churches, pastors, sermons, books, beliefs)
- Community-focused feature set
